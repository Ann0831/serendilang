About Serendilang

Last updated: October 28, 2025 (UTC)

Serendilang is an independent language exchange platform built by a solo developer with a simple belief — that technology should bring people closer, not further apart. The name “Serendilang” comes from “Serendipity” and “Language” — a reminder that the best connections often happen by chance, when people truly communicate. It was created to make genuine communication across languages and cultures easier — a place where you’re welcome, whether you come to practice a language or to meet new friends.

Our Vision

Serendilang is designed to help people meet through curiosity and learning — a space where serendipity, the beauty of unexpected encounters, can happen naturally.

Every feature — from posts and chats to voice and video calls — is built to encourage meaningful exchange and authentic connection.
