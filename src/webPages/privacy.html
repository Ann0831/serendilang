Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 4, 2025 (UTC)

This Privacy Policy explains how Serendilang ("we", "our", or "the Platform") collects, uses, and protects your personal information. By using Serendilang, you agree to the terms of this Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Platform.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to operate and improve our services:

  • Account information: username, language preferences, and profile picture.
  • OAuth data: basic information from Google or Apple login, such as your email and public profile image.
  • Usage data: IP address, browser type, device info, and interaction logs for performance and security.
  • Content data: messages, posts, or uploaded media shared within the Platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Platform and its features.
  • Authenticate users and prevent fraud or abuse.
  • Enable social interactions such as messaging and voice/video communication.
  • Analyze usage trends and optimize performance.
  • Comply with legal obligations when required by law.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share limited information in the following cases:

  • With trusted service providers (such as hosting or analytics) who help us operate the Platform.
  • When required by law, regulation, or government request.
  • With your explicit consent.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide our services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time through your account settings. Once deleted, your data will be removed from active systems within a reasonable timeframe.

5. Security

We use encryption, secure communication (HTTPS), and strict access control to protect your information. However, no online service can be completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

6. Cookies

Serendilang uses strictly necessary cookies to operate essential features of the Platform. These cookies are required for processes such as account registration, login, and maintaining your authenticated session. They do not contain personal sensitive data and are not used for advertising or tracking.

Specifically, we use cookies to:

  • Support the registration process, including authentication through Google OAuth.
  • Keep you logged in during your session (session token).
  • Protect your account from unauthorized access.

Some third-party login providers (such as Google) may set their own necessary cookies during the authentication process. These are required to complete registration and login, and are not used by Serendilang for analytics or advertising.

We do not use cookies for analytics, marketing, or cross-site tracking.

7. Real-Time Communication

Serendilang uses WebRTC technology to enable real-time voice and video calls between users. During the connection process, temporary network information such as IP addresses may be exchanged between participants through the ICE and STUN process to establish a connection. This exchange is currently required for peer-to-peer connectivity.

When a call is made, Serendilang may record technical information such as the participants’ IP addresses and timestamps for security, abuse prevention, and connection quality analysis. This data is used solely for maintaining service reliability and detecting misuse, and is not shared with other users or external parties.

8. Children’s Privacy

Serendilang is not intended for users under 16 years old (or the minimum legal age in your country). If we become aware that a minor has registered without consent, we will delete their data promptly.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed on servers located outside your country, including in regions such as the United States or Taiwan. We take reasonable measures to ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws.

10. Public Content

Posts you create on Serendilang are publicly visible on this Platform. Please do not share sensitive personal information in posts.

Private messages and voice/video calls are not publicly visible and are only shared with the participants involved.

11. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal data.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing (where applicable).

To exercise these rights, please contact us via the email listed below.

12. Updates to This Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page with its effective date. Continued use of the Platform means you accept the updated Policy.

13. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@serendilang.com .
